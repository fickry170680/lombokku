Morning after finish breakfast we are leaving the hotel and straight to Malimbu cliff 25 meters above sea level where here you stop to take a picture you can see the view below the cliff where there under the ridge there is a bat cave nice valley then to Malaka cliff 50 meters above sea level here you can walk up and down to see how beautiful panorama under water .reef corals, beautiful valley and you can see three islands Gili trawangan,Meno and Air.

Continue to Tanjung market to see activities people sell and buy something after that we’ll going to Sendanggile water fall 600 meters above sea level on the foot of Rinjani Mountain the biggest water fall in Lombok lies 300 meters below friendly local guide will escort and explaining you while walking down to the first water fall just 15 minutes on the way to the first water fall if you lucky you’ll see ( black and grey monkeys ) and if you fell enough strong you may continue to second water fall just 30minutes walking down in the forest & river enjoy the splash of cold water and under the water fall there is a pool you can swim and bathe as your wish back to have lunch in the local and simple restaurant.

Then we are going to Traditional house Bayan here you’ll see daily activities of the people how their life everyday on the way back to hotel you stop at Pusuk for feeding the monkeys and back to hotel.
