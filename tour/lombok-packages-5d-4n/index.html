Rundown : Airport/Seaport - Senggigi Resort Hotel – Kuta Beach Tour - Waterfall Tour – Gili Nanggu Sudak Kedis 3 Islands Tour – Airport/Seaport.

Day 1 : Lombok international Airport /seaport – Hotel

Greeting and meeting service upon arrival hotel drop off and free at your leisure time, dinner at Local Restaurant in down town Senggigi Area.

DAY 2 : KUTA BEACH TOUR ( 08 hours )

after finish breakfast our Tour guide will pick you up at the reception & we straight to Banyumulek village where here famous with pottery (Handmade) than we continue to Sukarara village (hand weaving) after that we will visit to Sade village (traditional house of Lombok) we keep going to Kuta beach here you can having lunch before you going to swim or relax on the beach enjoy the sun you may spend your time all day when you say enough & happy than we drop you back to the hotel.

suggestions :
please! Bring: shorts and T-shirt, towel, slippers, sun block, hat, sun glasses camera etc...

DAY 3 : WATERFALL TOUR ( 08 hours )

Morning after finish breakfast our Tour guide will greeting & pick you up than we straight to see the monkey forest here you can feeding them don’t worry all the monkeys going to school because they don’t like your money during the trip you can take a picture the terrace paddy o rice field when we arrive to the gate of waterfall the local guide he will Welcome you & take you just 08 minutes to the first waterfall we called ‘ Benang Setukel’ here just take a picture than continue to the second waterfall just 20 minutes walking during the trip you can see plantation such as : banana Coffee & coco amazing because so nature & beautiful spend your time here it is your paradise because the water flowing from the spring of Rinjani mountain you can swimming in the crystal water clean & so cold Lunch Box on the sport end program & back to the hotel.

Suggestions:
Please! Bring shorts and T-shirts, towel, sun glasses, hat slippers camera etc…

DAY 4 : GILI NANGGU SUDAK KEDIS TOUR 3 islands ( 08 hours )

After finish breakfast we are going to see you at the lobby and we straight to drop you to the port than we crossing to the Gili island by boat approximately 20 minutes first we visit at Gili kedis here you can swim & snorkeling enjoy the beautiful beach sleep on table beach while reading book to sit in the sun & you can do swing here when you say enough we will take you to Gili Nanggu here also you spend your time for snorkeling feeding the fish by toast they will welcome you & all of them said’ breakfast is coming when you fell hungry we will go to last island we called’ Gili Sudak here you can have Lunch in the simple Restaurant we serve the grill fish o fried rice to killing your time you may hanging around the island when you said’ we are Happy than we drop you back to the hotel.

Suggestion:
Please! Bring shorts and T-shirt sun block towel, hat, sun glasses, camera etc..

DAY 5 :  free program and we drop you off to the Airport/Seaport
